# NVIDIA Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Information

### What is NVIDIA?
NVIDIA is a global technology company specializing in designing and manufacturing graphics processing units (GPUs), system on a chip units (SoCs), and artificial intelligence accelerators for gaming, professional visualization, data centers, and automotive markets.

### When was NVIDIA founded?
NVIDIA was founded in 1993 by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem.

### Where is NVIDIA headquartered?
NVIDIA's headquarters are located in Santa Clara, California, USA.

## Products and Technology

### What are NVIDIA's main product lines?
- GeForce (Gaming GPUs)
- RTX (Ray tracing technology)
- Quadro (Professional visualization)
- Tesla (Data center and AI computing)
- GRID (Virtual GPU technology)
- Jetson (Edge AI computing platform)
- DRIVE (Autonomous vehicle technology)

### What is CUDA?
CUDA (Compute Unified Device Architecture) is NVIDIA's parallel computing platform and programming model that enables dramatic increases in computing performance by harnessing the power of the GPU.

### What is DLSS?
DLSS (Deep Learning Super Sampling) is NVIDIA's AI rendering technology that increases graphics performance using dedicated AI processors on RTX GPUs called Tensor Cores.

## AI and Deep Learning

### How is NVIDIA involved in AI?
NVIDIA provides hardware and software platforms for AI development, including GPUs optimized for deep learning, the CUDA-X AI libraries, and platforms like NVIDIA AI Enterprise.

### What is NVIDIA Omniverse?
NVIDIA Omniverse is a platform for connecting and creating 3D virtual worlds, enabling collaboration and simulation across multiple software applications and users.

## Gaming

### What are RTX GPUs?
RTX GPUs are NVIDIA's gaming graphics cards that feature ray tracing technology, which creates realistic lighting effects in real-time gaming.

### What is G-SYNC?
G-SYNC is NVIDIA's adaptive sync technology that synchronizes the refresh rate of a compatible monitor with the frame rate output of an NVIDIA GPU, reducing screen tearing and stuttering.

## Business and Support

### How can I contact NVIDIA customer support?
Customer support can be reached through the NVIDIA website's support section, where you can access knowledge base articles, driver downloads, and contact options.

### Does NVIDIA offer developer resources?
Yes, NVIDIA provides extensive resources for developers through the NVIDIA Developer Program, including SDKs, documentation, and forums.

### Where can I find NVIDIA drivers?
The latest NVIDIA drivers can be downloaded from the official NVIDIA website at https://www.nvidia.com/drivers or through the GeForce Experience application.
